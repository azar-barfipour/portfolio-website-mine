my, myself and I

First time in my life that I could choose my major, it was at the end of highschool which I chose Software Engineering. I was really curious about computers and I learned many various aspects of this field during my four years at university. In the last year of study there was a workshop about web developing, we created a simple website. It was my first time creating a website and it was very interesting when I saw the very first Hello World on my screen.

After graduation I joined a big company and I worked there for more than four years. It was a great opportunity to collaborate with more than 40 teammates and handle more than 100 clients, debugging, testing and developing forms by using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and SQL Server. However I gained huge experiences there but still I had a passion for creating websites.

Then, I moved to Canada to achieve my old goal which is frontend development. I started self-studying web development and joined CICCC college and gained many valuable and updated knowledge of web developing such as SASS, React.js and Ecma6.
